The Aquatic Messenger VR

How fun would it be to move freely under the water, and travel between defferent era? What about interacting with plankton and creatures? This is a game that provides such experience in VR. This project has an official google site.

I joined the Aquatic Messenger Team in 2018 as one of the developers. The team worked with local biologists to create models of each creature, and a holographic UI to display info of the grabbed creature.

I worked on fixing some materials and shaders, and the fish spawning algorithm. I really enjoyed working with the team. This was my first VR project, and I learned a lot from everything: programming, project exhibition, VR mechanics and a lot more.

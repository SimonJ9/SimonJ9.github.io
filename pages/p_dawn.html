The Dawn

This was my first project using Unreal Engine 4. We created a mini cooperative FPS that allows at most 3 players to play together. The goal is to bring ship to the destination. There are enemies showing up in the way, and each player can either stay on the ship or temporarily leave the ship for a battle. At least one player needs to drive the ship to make it move.

The team consisted of 3 programmers. I chose to be the "artist" and experimented with the terrain system and foliage of UE4. I modelled the ship in MAYA, and then worked on the lighting and post processing.

What I worked on

  • Terrain modelling
  • Ship modelling
  • Lighting and material dev

Gameplay

I have worked as a generalist developer on several projects, from commercial games to educational simulation. I have experience in developing gameplay logics in Unity and Unreal Engine, and optimizing for multiple platforms including PC, Linux, Webgl and IOS.

Asset Integration

I work closely with designers and artists, and provide custom tools depending on project needs. Art asset integration could always be a challenge, and I strive to make process as smooth as possible. During my previous work, I worked on shaders, animation tools, asset management tools and so on.

Networking

Sometimes the project requires server authorization. I have gained some experience in implementing player state preservation with NoSQL Databases, real time data broadcasting with UDP and websocket, and custom Http Client for game engines.
